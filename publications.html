COVID-19 Model Related Publications

Our automated data-driven model allows the capture of local transmission conditions and their change on the course of the SARS-CoV-2 epidemic at the county level. The model helps local public health and government officials “try out” different intervention measures (lockdowns, social distancing, quarantines) to understand how they might affect the timing and number of COVID-19 cases and deaths and the demand for hospital beds, ICU care, ventilators and other services at the county level. These predictions will help officials make decisions to best prepare the local health system so that it is not overloaded and will be able to manage these demands.
